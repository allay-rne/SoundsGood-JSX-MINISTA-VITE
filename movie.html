Boy in striped pajamas

The commandant's son befriends prisoner Shmuel through the fence. Tragedy.

Detailed movie information

Description

"The Boy in the Striped Pajamas" — a tragic 2008 drama about Bruno, the son of a concentration camp commandant, who befriends Jewish boy Shmuel through the fence. Their innocent wartime friendship ends in heartbreaking catastrophe, showing the horrors of war through a child's eyes.
